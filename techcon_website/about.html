Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for tech enthusiasts and has since grown into one of the largest global tech conferences. Over the years, we have welcomed thousands of participants from all corners of the tech world.

Our Mission

TechCon is committed to driving innovation and collaboration in the tech industry by bringing together developers, leaders, and innovators to share knowledge, spark ideas, and inspire action.

Past Speakers

Our previous conferences have featured leading figures in technology, including:

  • Jane Smith - AI researcher and keynote speaker in 2022. She discussed ethical AI and the future of machine learning.
  • Alan Johnson - CTO of FutureTech, shared insights on cloud scalability and infrastructure in 2021.
  • Lina Chen - Cybersecurity expert, talked about data protection and privacy in the digital age in 2020.
